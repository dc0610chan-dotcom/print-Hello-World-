✦ Project Overview

Find a career direction that matches who you are

This website is meant to support high school students who are still unsure about what career path they may want to pursue in university and beyond. Instead of guessing blindly, users can take an interactive quiz that helps them reflect on what they are interested in, where they naturally do well, and how they are doing academically.

By bringing together interests, strengths, and academic habits, the quiz is designed to guide users toward career directions that feel more realistic, personal, and worth exploring further. The goal is not to force one perfect answer, but to help students understand themselves better and feel more confident about their next steps.

Website mission

Helping students explore possible career paths

The homepage should show users that this project is about career discovery. Through the quiz, students can learn more about their interests, strengths, and academic performance to better understand what paths may fit them.
