OpenDoors

This website is meant to support high school students who are still unsure about what career path they may want to pursue in university and beyond. Instead of a choice being made for them, users can take an interactive quiz that helps them reflect on what they are interested in, where they naturally do well, and what careers they might enjoy exploring.

By bringing together interests, strengths, and academic habits, the quiz is designed to guide users toward career directions that feel more realistic, personal, and worth exploring further. The goal of OpenDoors is not to force one perfect answer, but to help students understand themselves better and feel more confident about their next steps.

Hi there. My name is Davin. I was originally studying Business Administration, but I am now switching over to Computer Science after discovering what I truly enjoy once I got into university. M

Mikaela

Back-End Logic

Hi! I’m Mikaela, a first-year Computer Science student with a minor in Business Administration at Laurier University. While figuring out my post-secondary path, I bounced between majors — psychology, engineering, English, chemistry — trying to find my fit. No online test or in-school guidance really helped me, and everyone seemed to have their own vision for my future, not mine. That’s why I believe a platform like OpenDoors is so important: a space to explore yourself, connect with others, and discover the many paths that exist.
